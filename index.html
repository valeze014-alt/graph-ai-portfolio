About the course

An intensive 8‑week, live cohort on applying Generative AI to real research and work—hands‑on and tool‑first. Includes weekly workshops, help sessions, private community, capstone coaching, feedback on assessments, a shareable certificate, and lifetime access to tutorials.

What I’m learning

Module 1 — Websites, Documents & Presentations

LLM fundamentals; generating Markdown/LaTeX/HTML; creating simple research sites; structured documents; AI‑assisted presentations.

Module 2 — AI‑Automated Data Analysis

Code Interpreter, Julius & TheBricks; file transforms; dashboards; automated reports; spreadsheet automations.

Module 3 — GenAI for Research & Text Analysis

Literature review tools (Elicit, LiteRev); text classification; sentiment; knowledge maps; summarization pipelines; information synthesis.

Module 4 — Custom GPTs & Chatbots

Chat architecture, context windows, RAG; custom GPTs; Botpress chatbots; connecting knowledge bases.

Module 5 — AI for Task Automation

Email responses, browser agents, multi‑step flows, agent‑based systems for repetitive research tasks.

Module 6 — Communication & Outreach

AI‑generated summaries, figures, video abstracts; visualization prompts; content scheduling.

Module 7 — Capstone & Showcase

Project progress checks, peer feedback, final presentation and Q&A.
